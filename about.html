The Story Behind QR Lab

QR Lab was born out of a shared frustration within our team. We noticed that the internet was flooded with "free" QR code generators that would secretly expire users' codes after a few days, holding their destination links hostage for a monthly subscription fee.

We knew there was a better way. We architected QR Lab to be a truly free, pro-grade utility. By engineering the platform to perform all generation locally in the user's browser, we eliminated server costs and data privacy concerns in one move. The result is a lightning-fast generator capable of producing print-ready, non-expiring QR codes for everyone.
